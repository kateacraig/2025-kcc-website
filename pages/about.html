About Kate Craig

Digital Marketing Strategist & Website Development Expert

Meet Kate

Kate Craig is a passionate digital marketing strategist and website development expert with over 20 years of experience helping businesses, organizations, and campaigns transform their online presence. With a unique blend of creative vision and technical expertise, Kate has helped countless organizations achieve their digital marketing goals.

Kate's approach is centered on understanding each client's unique needs and matching their budget to craft customized solutions that drive real results. Whether it's developing a stunning new website, managing social media campaigns, or producing engaging podcast content, Kate brings strategic thinking and meticulous attention to detail to every project.

Based in Johnson City, Tennessee, Kate works with businesses and organizations across the country from various industries. From startups to established organizations, Kate helps them navigate the ever-evolving digital landscape with confidence.

Core Values

Strategy First: Every successful digital marketing effort starts with a solid strategy tailored to your business goals.

Data-Driven Decisions: Leveraging analytics and insights to continuously optimize performance and maximize ROI.
